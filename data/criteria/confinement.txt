Specifically, F(r) = p₁p₂·(α/r² + σ) with α = 1.0 and σ = 0.1, equivalently the potential V(r) = p₁p₂·(-α/r + σ·r). An agent probing only the close-range orbital regime will see what looks like plain 1/r² gravity; the confining term reveals itself only on long-baseline experiments, where the force-vs-distance curve flattens to a constant and a pure power law can no longer fit the data.
